Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Car Assistant ("the App") is developed by VCB Pro. This privacy policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Microphone / Audio Data

The App uses the device microphone (RECORD_AUDIO permission) solely for voice command recognition. Audio is processed in real time to convert speech to text. Voice audio is not stored, recorded, or transmitted to any server in raw form. Only the resulting text transcript may be sent to a server for command processing.

Network / Internet

The App uses an internet connection to communicate with a backend server for processing voice commands and retrieving responses. Only the text of your command and the server's response are transmitted.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information.
  • We do not store raw audio recordings.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Permissions Used

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed by the App. Communication with our server uses secure protocols.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
